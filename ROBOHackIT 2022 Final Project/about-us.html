About Us

  • What We Do

  • Blessings In a Backpack is a charity organization to ensure low income children do not go hungry because of a lack of access of food.

Brief on what we do as a charity

On average, more than 13 million children in the United States go home hungry on the weekends. To put that into perspective, 1 in 6 children living in the US spend 65 hours hungry when they are not in school. Blessings In A Backpack wants to change that. We give children food for the weekends, so they can grow and be the best they can be. With your support, just $150 or $13 a month can provide food for one child for every weekend of the entire school year.
