Our Mission

We believe that no child should be hungry. Therefore, we make it our mission to meet once a week to fill and pack bags with a surplus of food. We do not get to see the smiles of the children when they recieve their food; however, we know what we do is causing that happiness and that is what motivates us to continue the Blessings in a Backpack mission here at Lambert High School.

How can you help?

  • If you are an LHS student, help pack bags in Room 1142
  • You can donate by visiting the official BIB website(learn more on the About Us page)
  • Go to the Get Involved Page on the BIB website and find volunteering opportunities there!
